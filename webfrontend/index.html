    Best Eats

    "In the 1960’s, when our founder, Gino Aioni, immigrated to America from Palermo, Sicily, he brought with him a special gift which was to shape the lives of his family for generations to come. His gift was a passion. Gino loved to cook, and when Gino cooked people came from far and wide to eat. There was never an empty seat at Gino’s table. In 1975, after raising two fine sons, Gino fulfilled his lifelong dream and opened the doors of the very first Gino’s Pizza Restaurant in Corona Del Mar California. The rest, as they say, is history in the baking!"
